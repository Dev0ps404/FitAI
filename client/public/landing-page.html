Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI adjust my workouts? expand_more
Our engine uses a proprietary model that tracks your sets, reps, and RPE (Rate of Perceived Exertion) alongside wearable data like sleep quality and resting heart rate. If your recovery is low, it will automatically adjust volume to prevent overtraining.
Do I need specific equipment? expand_more
No. FITAI offers tracks for bodyweight-only training, home gyms, and full commercial gym setups. You simply tell the AI what equipment you have available.
Can I sync my existing fitness watch? expand_more
Yes, we integrate with Apple Watch, Garmin, Whoop, Oura, and all major platforms via HealthKit or direct API connections.
